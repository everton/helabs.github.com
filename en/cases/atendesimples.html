SEEKING A PERFECT PARTNER

Why HE:labs?

Atende Simples has a very complex operation, involving lots of technologies, companies and systems. The voice platform was the responsibility of VoxAge, in São Paulo. It was necessary to decide the best way to create our web application:

  • Hire developers
  • Hire freelancers
  • Hire a development company

The first two options had various risks:

  • Would need someone to control the work of developers, putting weight on the structure early on the company.
  • There was a possibility of being dependent on the freelancers during a heated market.
  • The effort to replace individual developers or freelancers who leave the project would be considerable.

A TELCO MIDDLEWARE

For some tasks, the team had to study specifics of the application domain. The telecommunications back-end is a example. We were building a telco middleware in the cloud. Behind it, the system was composed in large 3 blocks:

  • A web interface, for the final users, where everything had to be simple. Here is where all end-users interact with the service.
  • A mid-field acting as the glue between the settings and tweaks by the end-user, and the telco services. This glue is a web service that runs on top of a physical telecommunications infrastructure for performance reasons.
  • The phone and voice system itself, which is developed by VoxAge.

A USER ACTIVATION FUNNEL SIMPLY MAGICAL

Atende Simples is being used by companies of different sizes and in all areas. It has sophisticated features such as call recording, control of operating hours of the service, call blocking, reports... all with great simplicity! Configuring an account is done in 6 easy steps:

  1. Choose a business phone: 0800 or national number
  2. Register your email and password
  3. Assign phones to receive calls
  4. Choose the type of service the public will hear when calling, whether a custom menu or automatic
  5. Your welcome message in text or an audio file
  6. Choose what will happen with calls

Done! It's working!
